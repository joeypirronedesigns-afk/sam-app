Common questions
What exactly is a "run"? +
A run is one press of any SAM tool's button — Run The Pulse, Generate ideas, Build 7-day plan, etc. Free gives you 10 tool runs and 3 full Content Playbooks. Creator gets 30 runs and 5 playbooks. Pro and Studio are unlimited.
Is the quality as good as hiring a real strategist? +
SAM is built on Claude — one of the most advanced AI models available. It doesn't just write — it thinks, plans and strategizes before it writes a word. The outputs are built for your specific story, your specific audience and your specific platforms. That's what makes it different from every other AI writing tool.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — cancel in one click, no questions, no fees. If you cancel within 7 days of your first charge, you get a full refund automatically. No emails required.
Does SAM post content for me? +
SAM writes the strategy — the hook, the script, the captions, the hashtags. You film it, edit it, and post it. SAM is your creative director, not a posting bot. That authenticity is what makes the content perform.
What platforms does SAM support? +
TikTok, YouTube (long-form and Shorts), Instagram Reels, Facebook Reels, LinkedIn, and X (Twitter). SAM tailors the hook, caption length, hashtag count, and posting strategy for each one differently.
I'm not a big creator. Is SAM for me? +
SAM is built specifically for people who are figuring it out — not the ones who already have a team. Business owners, solo creators, coaches, influencers. If you have something worth saying and you're not sure how to say it, SAM closes that gap.
What's a lead magnet and why does SAM build one? +
A lead magnet is a free downloadable resource your audience trades their email address to receive — a checklist, planner, guide or workbook. SAM builds one for you automatically with every Content Playbook, branded with your name and logo. It's how you turn viewers into subscribers. No other AI content tool does this.
What languages does SAM support? +
SAM writes natively in 12 languages — English (US, UK, Australian and Canadian), Spanish, French, Portuguese, German, Italian, Dutch, Japanese, Korean, Hindi, Arabic and Mandarin. It doesn't translate after the fact. It thinks and writes in your language from the start.

How do you feel most comfortable telling this story?
Your delivery style shapes your entire script. A narration script sounds completely different from an on-camera script. There's no wrong answer — the right answer is whatever lets you show up most authentically. Your voice is an asset. If people have ever complimented how you tell a story, lean into that.
When you tell a story out loud, you tend to...
How fast you naturally speak determines how long your video will be — and that determines which platforms it's right for. SAM uses this to set realistic expectations and build a script that actually fits your natural rhythm. A script that fights how you talk will never sound right on camera.
